Unleash the full potential of your multi-currency forex card by linking it to our companion mobile app If you have received your forex card, follow the instructions below to link it with our mobile app

Step 1

Download The BookMYForex Mobile App

Download the BookMyForex app Create an account yourself after providing your basic information

Step 2

Login using Registered Contact Details

Enter your registered phone number and email during the login process

Step 3

Navigate to Cards Section

Click on the "Cards" icon in the bottom bar

Step 4

Start Card Linking Process

Click on the Link a Card In Your Possession button

Oops! You have no cards to manage. Would you like to Issue a new card Or add a card that is already in your possession

Step 5

Enter Your Kit Number

Enter the kit rumber. Which is a 9 - 12 diet found on the bottom of the back side of your cord

Step 6

Create Your App Pin

You will need to enter create a mobile pin (app pin) which you will use to login to the app and to authorize certain card management operations after you have linked your card

Enter a 6 digit app pin and confirm the same by entering it again

Step 7

Optionally Enable Biometric Login

You may also use your fingerprint or facial recognition data to login to the app instead of typing the app pin. If so, please enable biometric login
